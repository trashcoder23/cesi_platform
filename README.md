CESI – Climate & Environmental Structural Index
CESI is a climate intelligence platform that transforms fragmented environmental indicators into a unified structural resilience score for global cities. The system integrates climate stress data, infrastructure vulnerability, and predictive modeling to identify cities at risk of climate-driven instability.

Instead of displaying isolated metrics like AQI or temperature, CESI provides a composite structural risk index that enables forward-looking climate analysis and smarter urban resilience planning.

🧠 CESI Framework
The Climate & Environmental Structural Index (CESI) integrates multiple environmental indicators into a unified structural resilience metric.

CESI combines:

Air Quality Volatility
Temperature & Heatwave Stress
Infrastructure Exposure
Instability Probability Modeling
Momentum-based Risk Acceleration Detection

Lower CESI scores indicate higher structural climate risk, while higher scores indicate greater resilience.
